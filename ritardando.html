Ritardando & Rallentando

1. Ritardando

(especially as a direction) with a gradual decrease of speed. "the first crotchet should be played somewhat ritardando"

2. Rallentado

(especially as a direction) with a gradual decrease of speed. "the cadenza is sung rallentando and greatly diminuendo"
